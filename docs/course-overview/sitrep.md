## The Scenario

You work for **Redback Systems** — a mid-sized Australian technology company with 200 staff across three offices. The IT team is small: two sysadmins, one network engineer, and a rotating helpdesk. You are a junior SOC analyst on secondment from a managed security provider.

Over the past 18 weeks, Redback Systems experiences a series of incidents. Each week you respond to a new development in the same ongoing case. By Week 18, you've built a complete picture of the breach and the skills to prevent the next one.

## Legal and Ethical Framework

All lab work in this course takes place on isolated networks you have explicit authorisation to test. The skills you learn are dual-use — the same knowledge that makes you effective also makes misuse illegal.

Relevant legislation:

- **Criminal Code Act 1995 (Cth)** — unauthorised access to computer systems
- **Cybercrime Act 2001 (Cth)** — unauthorised modification of data
- **Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)** — handling of personal information
- **Notifiable Data Breaches scheme** — mandatory breach reporting

Ignorance of the law is not a defence. Authorisation is everything.
