## Competency Standards

This is a VET course. You are assessed as **Satisfactory** or **Not Yet Satisfactory** — not graded with marks.

Satisfactory means: you have demonstrated the performance criteria and provided sufficient evidence across the knowledge and performance evidence requirements.

If any component is Not Yet Satisfactory, you will be given one further attempt.
