## After Action Review

The AAR is not an assessment. It's an honest conversation about what happened over 18 weeks.

**Four questions:**

1. **What did we plan to do?** (What was the course supposed to be?)
2. **What actually happened?** (What did we actually do?)
3. **Why was there a difference?** (What worked, what didn't, what surprised us?)
4. **What do we do differently next time?** (Honest suggestions — these get noted)

This is the same structure used by military units, firefighters, and incident response teams after real operations. Your feedback shapes the course for next year's cohort.
